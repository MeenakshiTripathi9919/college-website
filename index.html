About Our Institute

Established in 1973, our institute conducts pioneering research in Ayurvedic medicine, covering the biogeographic zones of Upper Gangetic plain and Central Indian region.

Our College

Our institute possesses a vast area of 45.63 acres divided into three blocks: Block A (15 Acres), Block B (0.76 acres), and Block C (29.63 acres).

Recently upgraded from Regional to Central status, our land was donated by Mr. R.V. Dhulekar (President of The Servant of Nation Society, Jhansi) to the President of India through a gift deed on May 25, 1967, specifically for research in Ayurveda.
