Admission Process

Our admission process is designed to select candidates with a strong academic background and genuine interest in Ayurvedic sciences.

1
Application Submission

Complete the online application form and submit required documents including academic transcripts, ID proof, and passport-sized photographs.

2
Entrance Examination

Qualifying candidates will be invited to take a written entrance examination testing their knowledge in relevant subjects.

3
Personal Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be called for a personal interview to assess their motivation, aptitude, and suitability for the chosen program.

4
Admission Confirmation

Selected candidates will receive an admission offer letter. Confirm your admission by paying the required fees within the specified timeframe.
